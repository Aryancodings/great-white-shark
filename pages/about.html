My Story

Hi — I’m John O’Reilly, the guy who’s always had petrol in his veins and a half‑smile ready for a sale. I never planned to run a car dealership — I just loved cars, mechanics, and the smell of new rubber more than most people love coffee. Over the years I tinkered, learned from every breakdown, every stubborn engine, and every deal that almost fell through. I’ve driven everything from beaters to beauties, argued over paint jobs I didn’t need, and chased down parts nobody else could find. Through it all, I held tight to one belief: your ride should make you grin — and not because you’re worrying about it breaking down. Now I’m here showing off the collection I’m proud of, telling stories I shouldn’t, and making sure you never leave without enjoying the experience (or at least a good laugh). Let’s find your perfect car — and maybe swap a few tall tales along the way.

“I’m the guy between you and public transport. Deal with it.”
